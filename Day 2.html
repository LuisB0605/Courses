Toy Poodle

Toy poodles are smaller versions of poodles, so they are smaller than the average dog. Toy poodles are a dog breed. Toy poodles are a fun pet but also a companion, they're extremely intelligent and like to perform to do tricks and fashion. Toy poodles possess strong swimming abilities, and swimming is a good exercise for them to keep their body fit.

Care and feeding

Toy poodles require lots of grooming. Poodle fur must be brushed and washed with shampoo. Some owners blow dry their poodles. Toy poodles eat 1/3-1/2 cups of food a day depending upon their weight.

Colors

The Poodle has a wide variety of colouring, including white, black, brown, blue, gray, silver, café au lait, silver beige, cream, apricot, and red, and patterns such as parti-, abstract, sable, brindle and phantom.
